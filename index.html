Stefanos Yiassemides

Music Composer for Film & Media

About

Born in the beautiful Mediterranean island of Cyprus, Stefanos Yiassemides is a Greek/Cypriot media composer currently based in Bristol, UK. At the age of 13 he picked up his first guitar and started studying and practicing the art of music. Since then, playing and writing music has been his lifelong passion. For the past five years he has been training and developing his skills as a professional music composer and producer. To date, he produced music in a vast variety of styles and musical settings. Growing up as part of a family full of cinephiles, he always believed that the marriage between music and the visuals is one of the most beautiful kind of art that exists, thus pursuing a career as a film composer came natural to him. A natural born perfectionist, Stefanos always strives to achieve the best possible outcome no matter how strict or pressing a given timeline for composing and producing original music may be.

Education

Stefanos holds a Bachelor of Arts with Honors in Professional Musicianship from the British and Irish Institute of Modern Music (University of Sussex) and a Master of Arts in Music Composition for Film & TV from the University of Bristol. During his time in Cyprus, he studied under some of the most acclaimed musicians in the Island. After completing his first degree, through which he specialized in performance and songwriting, he returned to his mentors in Cyprus where he continued to develop his compositional, orchestration and production skills. After adequately sharpening up his skills locally, he went abroad for the second time, earning himself his Master's degree and setting the scene for a promising career in the world of music.
